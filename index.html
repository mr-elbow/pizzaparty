02 / The Process

1. Feed the Levain

2. Autolyse

3. Mix the Final Dough
4. Bulk Ferment

5. Divide & Proof

Gently tip the dough onto a floured surface. Divide into equal portions and use a circular motion to tension the dough into tight balls. Refrigerate for 24 to 72 hours.
